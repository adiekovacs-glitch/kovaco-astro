Hiring is how a company decides what it's going to become.

The practice

Build hiring capability before growth exposes the absence of it.

Most companies don't have a hiring problem. They have a hiring system that hasn't caught up with what they're trying to build. KovaCo. exists to close that gap — helping founders design recruitment practices that are rigorous, scalable, and aligned with the kind of company they're becoming.

The goal is not dependency. It's building a hiring capability the company can sustain long after the engagement ends.
